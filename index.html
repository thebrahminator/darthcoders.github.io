About Smartcap

Blind Guidance and Road Assistance System.

Blind people face a lot of challenges on a daily basis. We're trying to build a system that will help blind people navigate the roads, based on the Road Signs. It recognises the road signs, interprets these signs, and sends it as a voice command to the user.

Their challenge is also extended at household, where they're unable to detect the emotions of people. Hence, we've also built a system which recognises the emotions of the people sitting in front of them.

Project Demo

The Modules Covered Include:

  • Road Sign Detection.
  • Emotion Detection to value synthesis.
  • Value interpreter for figuring out the emotion.
